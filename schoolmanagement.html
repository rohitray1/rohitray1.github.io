Functions of School Management Website

  • Student Management: Handles student admission, records, class and section details.
  • Teacher Management: Maintains teacher profiles, subject allocation and attendance.
  • Attendance Management: Tracks daily attendance of students and teachers.
  • Academic Management: Manages classes, subjects, syllabus and academic calendar.
  • Examination Management: Controls exam schedules, marks entry and result generation.
  • Fee Management: Handles fee structure, online payments and due tracking.
  • Timetable Management: Creates and manages class and teacher timetables.
  • Homework & Assignment: Allows teachers to upload homework and students to submit assignments.
  • Parent Portal: Enables parents to view attendance, results and fee status.
  • Library Management: Manages book records, issue and return system.
  • Transport Management: Handles bus routes, drivers and student transport details.
  • Hostel Management: Manages hostel rooms, fees and student records.
  • Communication System: Sends notices, announcements, SMS and emails.
  • Report Generation: Generates academic, attendance and financial reports.
  • User & Security Management: Provides login system and role-based access control.
